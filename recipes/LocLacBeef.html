Loc Lac Beef

Description

The Loc Lac Beef is a vietnamese dish.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Diced red bell pepper
  • Diced yellow onion
  • Oyster sauce
  • Low-sodium soy sauce
  • Dark soy sauce
  • Sugar
  • Fish sauce
  • Black pepper
  • Frech Watercress

Steps

  1. Make the Marinade Sauce
  2. Marinate the Beef Cubes
  3. Sear the Beef
  4. Cook the Vegetables
  5. Stir fry
